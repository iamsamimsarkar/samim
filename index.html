Frontend Development

A developer who specializes in creating the visual and interactive aspects of a website or web application. They use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build user interfaces, ensuring they are user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing.

UI / UX Designer

A User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) designer focuses on the overall feel of a product and ensuring it is intuitive and enjoyable to use. They conduct user research, create wireframes and prototypes, and design the visual elements of an interface while considering usability and accessibility.

Content Manager

A strategic role involving the planning, creation, and management of digital content. Content Managers ensure that the information shared on various platforms is engaging, accurate, and consistent with the brand voice. They collaborate with writers, designers, and marketing teams to produce high-quality content.

Software Tester

A professional responsible for evaluating software applications to ensure they are free of defects and meet the required standards. They design and execute test cases, identify bugs, and work closely with developers to resolve issues, ensuring the software is reliable and performs well under various conditions.
